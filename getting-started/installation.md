# Installation

1. Purchase **LayerCraft** from Fab and add it to your Unreal Engine library.

2. Open your project, navigate to **Fab → LayerCraft → Add Project**.
<img src="images/library.png" style="max-width:100%; border-radius:6px;">,

3. In the Content Browser, locate the **LayerCraft** or **MasterCraft** folder under your project content.
<img src="images/content.png" style="max-width:100%; border-radius:6px;">
